## PROBLEM 

-WIFI is connected but websites are not opening

## ENVIRONMENT

-WINDOWS 10

## STEPS TAKEN TO SOLVE ISSUE

-Diagnose the issue with ping 8.8.8.8 and ping google.com

-enter 'Change adapter options',then right-click Ethernet then enter properties

-then enter IPv4
